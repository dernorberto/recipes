Source: Tartes, quiches, pizzas ISDB: 978-2-84831-121-0 Ingredients 500g salmon (skin and boneless) 4 small courgettes/zucchini 3 eggs 10cl cream 1 dash curry 3 thyme branches salt, pepper Preparation Punch holes with a fork in doe and pre-cook for 10min at 180degrees Wash courgettes and slice with a peeler into long thin strips Boil them in salted water for 3min and spread to dry on kitchen roll paper Cut the salmon in thin slices, add salt and pepper Beat the eggs with the cream and the curry, then salt and pepper mildly Lay the courgette and salmon slices on the pre-cooked doe in the form drop the cream mixture and spread the thyme on top cook for 30min serve as soon as out of oven
